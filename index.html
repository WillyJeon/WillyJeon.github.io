Hi I'm William, and welcome to my website!

Lights Out

A third-person, top-down shoot-em-up set in the world of the Dresden Files. You play as Harry Dresden and fight through hordes of zombies in order to turn the lights back on in the city of the Chicago.

Karnissa's Caslte

A third-person puzzle game set in the world of Ethshar. You have to go through a series of puzzles in the castle in order to escape. You have the ability to control multiple explorers as solve the riddles and puzzles in order get out of the castle.

The Great War

A tabletop game, where you and other players must defend against waves of enemies. Set in the world of Ethshar you and the other players need to utilize your cards and action points in order to strategically fight off the enemy monsters.

Gate Crash

A top-down, roguelike inspired by web novels such as Solo Leveling. You start off as a soldier who was one of the first people to experience the existence of a gate and are teasked to fight and kill the monsters coming from the gate. (Work in progress)

Icarus VR

A virtual reality game that has fast-paced gun shooting and skill-based bow and arrow mechanics. Players compete in sky islands, each with its own unique features and challenges. The game offers a variety of game modes, such as Target Frenzy, Quick Draw, and Archery, each having a server-side leaderboard.
